# Dimension 20.03: Quality-Cost Routing

## Purpose

Study whether models/tools are selected based on cost, latency, risk, quality, or fallback policy.

## Steps

1. Check whether multiple models are configured.
2. Identify routing criteria: cost, latency, quality, risk.
3. Inspect fallback chains.
4. Check whether routing decisions are traced.
5. Check whether routing policy is configurable.

## Evidence to Capture

- Multi-model config
- Router/routing criteria
- Fallback chain definitions
- Routing decision traces
- Routing policy config

## Questions

1. Are multiple model tiers available?
2. What criteria drive model selection?
3. Are fallback chains defined?
4. Are routing decisions observable?

## Rating

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1-3 | Absent, implicit, ad-hoc, or unsafe |
| 4-6 | Present but inconsistent, weakly documented, or fragile |
| 7-8 | Clear model with tests, explicit interfaces, and operational safeguards |
| 9-10 | Mature, durable, observable, extensible, and proven under failure or scale |

> Can cheap models handle simple requests while expensive models handle complex ones?

# Dimension 17.03: Isolation Observability and Escape Handling

## Purpose

Study whether sandbox actions are logged, policy violations detected, and escape attempts handled.

## Steps

1. Check whether sandbox actions are logged.
2. Inspect policy violation detection.
3. Check whether escape attempts are monitored.
4. Check whether violations trigger alerts.

## Evidence to Capture

- Sandbox audit logs
- Policy violation detectors
- Escape monitoring
- Alerting rules

## Questions

1. Are sandbox actions observable?
2. Are policy violations detected?
3. Are escape attempts handled?
4. Are violations alerted to operators?

## Rating

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1-3 | Absent, implicit, ad-hoc, or unsafe |
| 4-6 | Present but inconsistent, weakly documented, or fragile |
| 7-8 | Clear model with tests, explicit interfaces, and operational safeguards |
| 9-10 | Mature, durable, observable, extensible, and proven under failure or scale |

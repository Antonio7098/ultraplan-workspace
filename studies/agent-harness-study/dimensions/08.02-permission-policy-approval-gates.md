# Dimension 08.02: Permission Policy and Approval Gates

## Purpose

Study how sensitive operations are gated.

## Steps

1. Identify permission schema.
2. Inspect approval policy definitions.
3. Check how approval is persisted.
4. Check approval revocation mechanisms.
5. Look for policy tests.

## Evidence to Capture

- Approval states
- Confirmation handlers
- Permission metadata
- Policy engine integrations

## Questions

1. Which actions require approval?
2. Who can approve?
3. Are approvals scoped and expiring?
4. Can policy override model intent?

## Rating

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1-3 | Absent, implicit, ad-hoc, or unsafe |
| 4-6 | Present but inconsistent, weakly documented, or fragile |
| 7-8 | Clear model with tests, explicit interfaces, and operational safeguards |
| 9-10 | Mature, durable, observable, extensible, and proven under failure or scale |

> Can approval be granted narrowly rather than globally?

# Dimension 14.02: Approval Session Design

## Purpose

Study how approval is requested, persisted, scoped, resumed, timed out, and audited.

## Steps

1. Identify approval request flow.
2. Check whether approval sessions are persisted.
3. Inspect scoping (approve once vs approve all).
4. Check timeout behavior for pending approvals.
5. Check whether approvals are audited.

## Evidence to Capture

- Approval request handlers
- Session persistence
- Scope definitions
- Timeout config
- Approval audit logs

## Questions

1. How is approval requested?
2. Are approval sessions durable?
3. Can approvals be scoped?
4. Do approvals time out?
5. Are approvals audited?

## Rating

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1-3 | Absent, implicit, ad-hoc, or unsafe |
| 4-6 | Present but inconsistent, weakly documented, or fragile |
| 7-8 | Clear model with tests, explicit interfaces, and operational safeguards |
| 9-10 | Mature, durable, observable, extensible, and proven under failure or scale |
